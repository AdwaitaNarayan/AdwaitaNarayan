## About Me

I'm **Adwaita Narayan Behera**, a Software Developer with a strong focus on **Python backend engineering and applied AI systems**.

I specialize in building scalable backend services, REST APIs, and automation pipelines using **FastAPI, Flask, PostgreSQL, and SQLAlchemy**. I have hands-on experience working with **AI/ML and LLM-based systems**, including OCR pipelines, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), resume scoring engines, and intelligent assessment platforms.

Currently, I work as an **AI Developer at Hirekarma**, where I develop AI-driven hiring and assessment solutions such as automated evaluation engines, ATS resume scoring, domain classification, and job recommendation systems. Previously, during my internship at **AAANS Services Pvt. Ltd.**, I worked on PDF data extraction, OCR automation, and domain-specific LLM fine-tuning.

I enjoy solving real-world problems through clean backend architecture, efficient data pipelines, and practical AI integration ‚Äî with a strong emphasis on maintainability, performance, and scalability.
